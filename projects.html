Our Projects

Discover the impactful projects our committees are working on to support the SUCSA community.

Digital Hub - TechPulse Committee

The Digital Hub is an initiative by the TechPulse Committee aimed at empowering students with digital skills, access to technology, and innovative solutions for the future.

Poultry Project - Agricultural Committee

The Agricultural Committee is running a poultry project to promote food security and sustainable agricultural practices among students and the community.

Health Camp - Health Committee

The Health Committee organizes health camps to provide free medical check-ups, health awareness, and wellness programs to benefit both students and the community.

SUCSA Ushering Agency - Ushering Committee

The Ushering Committee runs the SUCSA Ushering Agency, a professional service that trains and provides ushers for events while creating employment opportunities for students.
