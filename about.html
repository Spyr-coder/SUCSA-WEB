Who We Are

We are a community of students from tertiary institutions who hail from Seme Constituency in Kisumu County. We unite to support one another, build strong networks, and grow together through shared opportunities.

Our mission is to empower our members through mentorship, leadership development, events, and collaborative resource sharing.
